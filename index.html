江艺烨 Yiye Jiang

I am currently a postdoc at Statify, joint team of Laboratoire Jean Kuntzmann and Centre Inria de l'Université Grenoble Alpes with Sophie Achard. I did my thesis under the supervision of Jérémie Bigot and Sofian Maabout at Institut de Mathématiques de Bordeaux. My research is at the interface of time series analysis, complex data analysis, and graph learning, where I develop new statistical models to respond to the challenges of continuously evolving data scenario from real applications. The models already developped span both frenquentist and bayesian approaches, with a focus ranging from methodology, computation, and application. My applications include brain functional connectivity networks inference, demorgraphic data analysis.
